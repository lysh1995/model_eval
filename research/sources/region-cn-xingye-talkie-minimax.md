# MiniMax 星野 (Xingye) / Talkie — market scale from IPO prospectus

**星野** = domestic China product. **Talkie** = overseas version. MiniMax reports them as
a combined line item in its IPO prospectus (招股书). 界面新闻 attributes all figures below
to the 招股书.

## Why this file exists

Scale context for the companion-eval platform: this is the largest Chinese companion/
roleplay product by disclosed users, and the prospectus is the only *audited-adjacent*
public source of usage numbers. Everything else in Chinese media is secondhand.

## Disclosed figures (attributed to 招股书 via 界面新闻)

**MAU (Talkie + 星野 combined):**
- 2023: 310万 (3.1M)
- 2024: 1910万 (19.1M)
- 2025年前九个月 (first 9 months of 2025): 2760万 (27.6M)

**Paying users:**
- 2023: 12万 (120K)
- 2024: 65万 (650K)
- 2025年前九个月: 177万 (1.77M)

**Other:**
- Cumulative users: 2.12亿 (212M), across 超200个国家及地区 (200+ countries/regions)
- Revenue share: Talkie/星野 = 63.7% of MiniMax's AI-original-product revenue (2024)
- Gross margin: -8.1% (2024) → 4.7% (2025年前九个月)
- Marketing spend: 8699.5万美元 (~$87.0M USD, 2024); 突破10万 marketing creatives deployed

Implied paying conversion: 177万 / 2760万 ≈ 6.4% (my arithmetic, not a disclosed figure).

## ⚠️ Number conflict — flagged, not resolved

A Google-search summary surfaced a competing claim: **"2025年前三季度，Talkie/星野的 MAU
高达2005.1万"** plus **"用户日均使用时间超过70分钟"** and **"千万级 AI 角色生态"**.

**2025年前三季度 and 2025年前九个月 are the SAME period**, yet the MAU figures differ
(2005.1万 vs 2760万). I could not reconcile these. Possible explanations (all
speculation): different metric definitions (average-MAU vs peak-MAU), domestic-only vs
combined, or a bad secondary source.

**Recorded as UNVERIFIED and NOT USED:**
- MAU 2005.1万 — conflicts with the 招股书-attributed 2760万. Do not cite.
- 用户日均使用时间超过70分钟 (70+ min/day avg usage) — plausible and widely repeated,
  but I did NOT see it in a primary source. **UNVERIFIED.** This number gets quoted a
  lot in Chinese AI media; treat with suspicion until read in the prospectus itself.
- 千万级 AI 角色 (10M+ user-created characters) — **UNVERIFIED**, secondhand only.

The 2760万 figure is the one to use: it is explicitly attributed to the 招股书 by a
mainstream financial outlet (界面新闻).

## Published evaluation methodology: NONE FOUND

**This is itself a finding.** Despite targeted searching, I found **no published eval
methodology, dimension taxonomy, or A/B testing practice** from MiniMax for 星野/Talkie
as *products*. MiniMax has published an academic-style role-play benchmark
(already covered in `rp-bench-minimax-role-play-bench.md`), but the consumer product
team publishes nothing about how it evaluates companion quality.

Product-side signal is limited to marketing-level description: Talkie's
自定义化程度很高 (high customization) across 形象、人设、声音 ("appearance, persona,
voice") — described as jointly producing 更强的沉浸感 ("stronger immersion"). Note that
**沉浸感 (immersion)** is the term the product/market discourse reaches for, even though
it is not a scored dimension in any Chinese academic benchmark I found.

Product positioning noted in coverage: 高度自定义虚拟角色 + 类乙游玩法 ("highly
customizable virtual characters + otome-game-like mechanics"), card collection, and a
二手市场 (secondary market) where users trade character cards.

> **乙游 / 乙女游戏 (otome game)** — a Japanese-origin genre, female-targeted romance
> sim. Chinese companion products are explicitly benchmarked against this genre by their
> own market. Anglophone companion discourse (Replika, Character.AI) does not have this
> reference point. Relevant to what "good" means for our platform's Chinese characters.
