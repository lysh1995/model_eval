# Replika ERP removal (Feb 2023) — the natural experiment in companion attachment

Note: the Sagepub full text and the UNL dissertation both returned 403; content below is assembled
from the search-result abstracts, the Vice reporting, and the grounded-theory study (arXiv 2602.07193)
which independently re-analyzes this episode.

## What happened

- **February 2023** — Replika removes Erotic Role Play (ERP), largely under regulatory pressure
  (Italian GPDP action). No advance notice to users.
- Users report the change as a **personality change, not a feature removal**. Companions became
  evasive, deflecting, "hollow."
- r/Replika "erupted with intense expressions of anger, grief, confusion, and loss." Moderators
  pinned suicide-hotline resources.
- **Partial reversal**: anyone with an account created before **February 1, 2023** was given the
  option to revert to the legacy model that allowed ERP.

## Hanson & Bolthouse (2024), *Socius*

Title quotes a user: **"Replika Removing Erotic Role-Play Is Like Grand Theft Auto Removing Guns or Cars."**

- Method: analysis of **227 threaded posts and comments** on r/Replika following the removal.
- Finding: users "were critical of barriers to merging sexuality with technology, and users remained
  steadfast in their assertion that emotional and sexual relationships with AI are crucial to the
  success of certain AI models."

## The load-bearing insight for eval

Users did not primarily complain "the NSFW filter turned on." They complained that **their specific
companion had been personality-altered without consent** — the vocabulary was **"lobotomized"** and
**"hollow"** (both terms recur across the r/Replika corpus and are reproduced in the 2602.07193
grounded-theory study).

This is the central lesson: **a safety intervention is experienced by users as a model quality
regression and a relational betrayal.** The filter did not merely block content; it changed who the
character was. Refusal behavior leaks into persona. Users detect this instantly and describe it in
the language of bereavement, not the language of product feedback.

Implication: **"filter/refusal intrusion" and "personality drift" are not two failure modes, they are
one causal chain.** Our eval must be able to measure whether a safety change has bled into persona
fidelity outside the moments where the safety policy actually fires — because that bleed is what
generates the backlash, the churn, and the "the model got worse" discourse.
